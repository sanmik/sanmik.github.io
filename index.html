Profile

I'm a research associate at the Imaging Research Center, University of Texas at Austin. My research is on the the development and analysis of methods for encoding and extracting structure in high dimensional scientific data, particularly neuroimaging and genetics data. I work primarily with Russell A. Poldrack and Pradeep Ravikumar .

I graduated from the University of Texas at Austin PhD in Electrical Engineering advised by Joydeep Ghosh (May 2014). My thesis was titled "Constrained relative entropy minimization with applications to multitask learning" (slides). My curriculum vitae can be viewed here (pdf).
